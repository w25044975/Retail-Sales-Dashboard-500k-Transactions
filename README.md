# 📊 Retail Sales Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

## Overview

This Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of retail sales performance using over **500,000+ transaction records**. The dashboard is designed to help business stakeholders monitor revenue trends, customer activity, product performance, and geographical sales distribution.

![Dashboard Screenshot](Snapshot%20of%20DashboardR.png)

---

## Business Objective

The objective of this dashboard is to:

* Track overall sales performance.
* Monitor customer purchasing behavior.
* Identify top-performing products.
* Analyze revenue trends over time.
* Compare sales performance across countries.
* Support data-driven business decisions.

## Dashboard Features

### 1. Revenue Trend Analysis

* Monthly revenue performance visualization.
* Identifies seasonal sales patterns.
* Tracks business growth over time.

### 2. Top Products Analysis

* Highlights the highest revenue-generating products.
* Helps identify best-selling items.
* Supports inventory and marketing decisions.

### 3. Revenue by Country

* Compares sales performance across different countries.
* Identifies key revenue-generating markets.
* Assists in geographical expansion strategies.

### 4. Interactive Filters

Users can dynamically analyze data using:

* Year-Month Slicer
* Country Slicer

## Insights Generated

### Revenue Performance

* Total revenue exceeded **£8 million**.
* Revenue peaked at approximately **£1.1 million** during November.

### Customer Activity

* Average order value remained strong at approximately **£440**.

3. MEDIUM CERAMIC TOP STORAGE JAR

The **United Kingdom** generated the majority of revenue, significantly outperforming other countries.
